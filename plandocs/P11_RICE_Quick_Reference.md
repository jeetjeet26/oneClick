## Quick Decision Framework

### When evaluating new product ideas:

**MUST SCORE:**
1. Calculate RICE score
2. Identify dependencies
3. Estimate effort realistically
4. Check resource availability

**DECISION RULES:**
- RICE > 50: Do immediately (Tier 0)
- RICE 25-50: Do in 2026 (Tier 1)
- RICE 10-25: Consider for 2027 (Tier 2)
- RICE < 10: Parking lot (Tier 3)

**EXCEPTION CASES:**
- Strategic importance overrides RICE
- Client commitment can accelerate timeline
- Technical dependency can block higher-RICE items
