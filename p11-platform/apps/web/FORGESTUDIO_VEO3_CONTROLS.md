## Tips for Maximum Quality

1. **For Audio-Enhanced Videos**:
   - Describe desired sounds in your prompt
   - Mention music style (e.g., "upbeat jazz", "calm ambient")
   - Include dialogue if needed (e.g., "friendly tour guide explaining")

2. **For Silent Videos**:
   - Disable audio to avoid unnecessary processing
   - Focus on visual details in your prompt
   - Plan to add custom audio in post-production

3. **Duration Selection**:
   - Start with 8 seconds for complex scenes
   - Use 4 seconds for simple, focused shots
   - Test different durations to find what works best

## Troubleshooting

**Q: Can I change duration after generation?**
A: No, you must select duration before generating. Plan accordingly.

**Q: Can I disable audio after generation?**
A: Yes, you can mute the video in post-production or download without audio track.

**Q: What if I need longer videos?**
A: Generate multiple clips and stitch them together. Veo 3 currently supports max 8 seconds per generation.

**Q: Is audio generated for all scenes?**
A: Yes, if enabled. Veo 3 intelligently adds appropriate audio based on your prompt.
